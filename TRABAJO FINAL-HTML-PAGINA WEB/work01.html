DEVELOPMENT OF A VIDEO GAME

The proposed project is a visual prototype in which the research and development of a video game in smartphone format is sought. It is a visual presentation of a video game called "Tetris with some Pizza", it will show how the idea could be developed in a mobile format, focusing on the aesthetic part. To visually show the idea of the video game, Adobe XD software has been used, which allows us to simulate a mobile screen and through layers, buttons... unite one screen with another as a mobile app.
