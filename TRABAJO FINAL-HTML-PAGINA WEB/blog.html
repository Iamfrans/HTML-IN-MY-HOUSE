MY LYFE

I am a 25 year old programmer living in Huanuco, Peru. Since I was young, I have always been interested in technology and computing, and decided to study a career in programming so that I could make my passion my job.

After graduating from university, I started working at a software development company in my hometown. There, I had the opportunity to work on exciting and challenging projects, such as the development of a mobile application for a logistics company and the design of an inventory management system for a chain of retail stores.

I love my job as a programmer as it allows me to solve problems and create innovative solutions for my clients. It always excites me when I find a unique solution to a problem that seems impossible to solve.
